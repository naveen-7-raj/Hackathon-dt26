📖 How to Read SHAP Values

⬆️
Positive SHAP

Feature pushes prediction toward Approved. Higher value = stronger positive influence.

⬇️
Negative SHAP

Feature pushes prediction toward Rejected. Larger magnitude = stronger negative pull.

📊
Mean |SHAP|

Average absolute SHAP across all predictions. Higher bar = more influential feature overall.
